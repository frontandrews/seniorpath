# Leadership Story Bank

## Goal

Map real project stories to leadership and ownership questions so you do not improvise during interviews.

## Story 1: Technical leadership under ambiguity

Use for questions like:

- tell me about a time you led a project
- tell me about a time you created clarity
- tell me about a difficult technical decision

Candidate project:

- Rogue Credit Union

## Story 2: Driving standards and adoption

Use for questions like:

- tell me about a time you influenced without authority
- tell me about improving engineering quality
- tell me about raising standards across teams

Candidate project:

- Viamo

## Story 3: Delivery and ownership

Use for questions like:

- tell me about owning something end to end
- tell me about a time you unblocked a team
- tell me about a time you had to ship under pressure

Candidate project:

- Alpha Omega / Timberdoodle

## Story 4: Leadership growth

Use for questions like:

- tell me about mentoring people
- tell me about leading a team
- tell me about building process

Candidate project:

- JCA
